Justice League

Superman

Superman, born Kal-El and legally named Clark Joseph Kent,is the last son of Krypton, sent as the dying planet's last hope to Earth, where he grew to become its protector

Batman

Batman is the superhero protector of Gotham City, a tortured, brooding vigilante dressed as a sort of human bat who fights against evil and strikes fear into the hearts of criminals everywhere.

Wonder woman

Wonder Woman is an Amazon warrior princess and one of the most powerful superheroes in the DC Universe. Her equipment includes the Lasso of Truth, magic gauntlets, and an invisible jet
